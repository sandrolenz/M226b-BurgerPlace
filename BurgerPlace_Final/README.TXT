PROJECT TITLE:Burgerplace
PURPOSE OF PROJECT:M226b
VERSION or DATE:22.01.2023
HOW TO START THIS PROJECT:Greenfoot
AUTHORS:Daniel Fankhauser
USER INSTRUCTIONS:

- Open Greenfoot, click on run and start to gain as much money as you can, by making burgers.
- You can drag the ingredients of the burger on the plate and then klick on the bell to finnish the order.
- The faster you are at making a burger, the more money you gain.
- If you take longer than 60 seconds to finnish a burger, you loose money!
- In case, you will ever make a mistake, just click on the trashcan. This will delete your current burger.
